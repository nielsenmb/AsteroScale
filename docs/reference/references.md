# References and further reading

Links below open the publication record in the
[NASA Astrophysics Data System (ADS)](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/). This is
not an exhaustive bibliography; the source docstrings identify the relation
used by each function.

## Introductory reviews

These reviews go well beyond AsteroScale, but are useful next steps:

- [Chaplin & Miglio (2013), *Asteroseismology of Solar-Type and Red-Giant Stars*](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2013ARA%26A..51..353C/abstract) — broad introduction and astronomical applications.
- [Hekker & Christensen-Dalsgaard (2017), *Giant star seismology*](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2017A%26ARv..25....1H/abstract) — evolved stars and mixed modes.
- [García & Ballot (2019), *Asteroseismology of solar-type stars*](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2019LRSP...16....4G/abstract) — observations, power spectra, and stellar interiors.
- [Aerts (2021), *Probing the interior physics of stars through asteroseismology*](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021RvMP...93a5001A/abstract) — a wider review covering several classes of pulsating stars.
